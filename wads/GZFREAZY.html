GZFREAZY.wad - Freazy's Ultimate Doom Recreation for GZDoom v0.1

I can't remember when I started work on this wad, but if I had to guess, I would probably say it started in about August 2021. If you've played the first episode of Ultimate Doom, and you likely have, then you'll recognise the general layouts of these maps. However, I've added several GZDoom exclusive mechanics and made them more difficult.

My motivation for recreating Knee-Deep in the Dead was to allow me to study the original Doom episode as a mapper. Making the wad for GZDoom allowed me to practice using advanced features, as well as giving me a chance to apply my ACS library to an actual map.

You will need GZDoom to play this wad. You will also need a copy of Doom 2 which you can buy on Steam or GOG.
